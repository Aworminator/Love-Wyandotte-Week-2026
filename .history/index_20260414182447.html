LOVE WYANDOTTE WEEK 2026

Come join us for love week!

Love Wyandotte Week is a citywide movement of churches, families, and neighbors coming together to serve, celebrate, and share hope throughout our community. From hands‑on service projects that meet real needs, to our free family event—the Bash at Bishop—and our outdoor worship gathering, this week is all about showing the love of Jesus in a meaningful and hands-on way throughout the community. Whether you volunteer, bring your family to the Bash, or join us for worship, you’re helping make Wyandotte a place where people feel seen, valued, and cared for. Sign up, show up, and be part of something that truly makes a difference.
